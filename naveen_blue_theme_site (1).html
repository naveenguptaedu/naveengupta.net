Workshops & Detailed Programs

Below are the signature workshops, bespoke programs and expert sessions offered by Dr. Naveen Gupta. Each program can be customised in duration (90 min / half day / full day / multi-day) and audience (teachers, students, school leaders, COEs, universities).

Comprehensive Workshop Catalog

1. Use of AI for Educators

Practical, classroom-ready AI for teachers: lesson planning with AI, AI-assisted assessments, personalised practice worksheets, AI ethics in schools, safe deployment strategies, and hands-on tool labs using ClassPoint, Magic School, Gamma, Fliki and Canva for Education. Outcomes: teachers design an AI-integrated lesson plan and create one personalised worksheet for their classroom.

2. Cyber Safety & Security

School-wide cyber safety strategy covering digital footprints, cyberbullying prevention, secure passwords & accounts, parental awareness sessions, incident reporting workflows, and establishing a Cyber Safety & Security Club. Outcomes: a school-ready cyber safety policy draft and classroom activities to teach digital responsibility.

3. Digital Tools for 21st Century Skills

Hands-on sessions on tools that amplify learning: collaborative platforms, formative assessment tools, interactive presentation tools, and digital portfolios. Focus on communication, collaboration, critical thinking and creativity (the 4Cs). Outcomes: teachers create a blended learning module applying at least two digital tools.

4. Gamification & Game-Based Learning

Designing learning experiences using gamified mechanics, badges, leaderboards, and game-based assessments. Practical activities using Quizizz, ClassPoint, and simple game-design using Scratch or Unity (intro). Outcomes: a gamified lesson plan and rubric for assessing game-based learning.

5. Life Skills & SEL (Social Emotional Learning)

>

Integrated life-skills workshops focusing on communication, collaboration, problem-solving, resilience and decision-making. Activities for classroom practice, parent engagement ideas and assessment strategies for soft-skills. Outcomes: SEL activities mapped to grade levels and an evaluation checklist for teachers.

6. National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 Implementation

Practical support for schools to align curriculum, pedagogy and assessment with NEP 2020. Sessions include competency-based learning, multidisciplinary projects, experiential learning strategies, and assessment reform. Outcomes: a one-term implementation roadmap for NEP-aligned classroom practices.

7. National Curriculum Framework — Secondary Education

Workshops tailored to secondary school teachers on implementing the National Curriculum Framework (NCF) at the classroom level: curriculum mapping, competency frameworks, learning outcomes and classroom resources. Outcomes: mapped curriculum units and sample lesson sequences.

8. Coding & Computer Science Track

Progressive coding programs from foundational to advanced: Python for classrooms (visualisation + problem solving), C++ & Java fundamentals for senior students, Database fundamentals (SQL) and Networking basics. Includes pedagogy for teaching programming, assessment strategies and sample projects. Outcomes: course outline for term-wise coding curriculum and sample lab assignments.

9. Expert Sessions for Class 12 — Board Exam Preparation

Focused revision sessions for CBSE Class 12 (CS/IP/AI): exam pattern breakdown, important topics, sample solved answers, time-management strategies, and mock practice sessions. Outcomes: concise revision notes and mock test paper with marking scheme.

10. Student Competitions & Events (Geek-a-Hertz)

Design, organise and mentor tech fests and competitions: app development, robotics (Battle Botz), game design, movie making, e-waste innovation, B-plan with technology and more. Outcomes: event blueprints, rubrics and volunteer training plans.

11. Teacher Professional Development & Leadership

Training-of-Trainers (ToT) programs, leadership workshops for school heads, and peer-mentoring program design. Outcomes: ToT certificate program structure and facilitator guides.

12. Parivartan — Community Digital Literacy

Programs to teach parents and communities basic digital skills to support student learning: device use, school portals, and simple digital safety. Outcomes: parent training modules and take-home resource packs.

13. Assessment & Feedback Innovations

Strategies for formative assessment, rubrics for project work, AI-assisted grading workflows and feedback loops that support growth mindset. Outcomes: sample rubrics and an implementation checklist.

14. Custom Consultancy & School Transformation

End-to-end consultancy for digital transformation—policy, teacher training, infrastructure assessment, community engagement and monitoring. Outcomes: school transformation roadmap and KPIs.
